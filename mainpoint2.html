Celebrities and Social Media

It is hard to detach yourself from something that has been a part of your life for many years and that has that amount of control. People go on social media for many different reasons so if you take away social media it may affect the person depending on what they used social media for. For example promotion, many people use social media to promote their work or themselves and detaching social media will allow people to lose out of income and fall into depression or anxiety. Detaching yourself from social media may have benefits or it may not depending on how you see it. It can cause less anxiety if it causes you anxiety or it can possible benefit you in ways such as gaining a following or gaining more income. Social media can help build you up or break you down, its up to you to fix.
