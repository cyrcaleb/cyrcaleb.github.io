How Social Media Impacts your Brain in Education

As many adolescents develop an addiction to social media, they become so consumed with always needing to be on social media that they are forced to multi-task. Multi tasking has led to more than just an extra distraction, but a short term memory problem. Students are not able to remember information as successful after using social media rather than when they don’t. This just shows another negative aspect that social media brings to a student’s life as they are becoming dependent on social media rather than solely focusing on their academics.
